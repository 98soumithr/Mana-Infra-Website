WHO WE ARE

Building India's Critical Infrastructure

Mana Infrastructure Private Limited is a trusted name in civil engineering and infrastructure construction. Founded in 2012 and incorporated as a private limited company in 2025, we have consistently delivered complex projects across highways, tunnels, irrigation, and bridges — on-time and within budget.

Headquartered in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, with offices in Hyderabad, Telangana, we operate across five states with a skilled workforce, an advanced equipment fleet exceeding 500 machines, and an unwavering commitment to quality and safety.
