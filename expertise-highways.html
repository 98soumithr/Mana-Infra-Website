What We Do

Mana Infrastructure specializes in national highway construction including six-lane and four-lane expressways over challenging terrain. Our expertise spans the entire lifecycle — from earthwork and subgrade preparation to bituminous paving, structures, and road furniture.

We have successfully delivered highway projects under NHAI's Bharatmala Pariyojana program, working across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Odisha with a reputation for on-time execution and quality workmanship.
