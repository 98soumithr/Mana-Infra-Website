What We Do

Mana Infrastructure delivers new gauge line construction for RVNL (Rail Vikas Nigam Limited) and Central Railway, including major bridges, RUBs (Road Under Bridges), ROBs (Road Over Bridges), station buildings, HI platforms, and COPs.

Our railway division handles everything from formation works and track laying to station infrastructure, bringing the same precision engineering and safety standards that define all our projects.
