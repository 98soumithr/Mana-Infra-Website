What We Do

Mana Infrastructure has extensive experience in tunnel construction using the New Austrian Tunnelling Method (NATM). We specialize in twin-bore tunnels up to 900 meters, executing complex underground works with precision and safety.

Our tunnel portfolio includes work on the Polavaram project — one of India's most significant irrigation initiatives — where we handle excavation, shotcrete lining, rock bolting, and waterproofing in challenging geological conditions.
